How to raise happy twins

Naomi Fujii

Raising twins brings a unique set of joys and challenges that can profoundly shape their emotional well-being and happiness. As a parent, nurturing the individual personalities and needs of each twin while fostering a strong, supportive bond between them is essential. By focusing on emotional intelligence, creativity, positive relationships, a growth mindset, and mindfulness, you can create an environment where your twins can thrive both individually and together.
